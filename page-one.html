The Sun

The Sun is the heart of it all. Everything in the Solar System orbits this enormous ball of superhot gas.

The Sun is 4.6 billion years old and can fit around 1.3 million Earths inside of it. The surface temperature is around 5,500Â°C with the core reaching unimaginable temperatures of about 15.5 million degrees C!

The Sun generates its energy to sustain its existence through nuclear fusion, which occurs in the superheated core under pressure 250 billion times greater than Earth's atmospheric pressure!

Did you know?

The Sun accounts for about 99.86% of the total mass of the Solar System!

It takes light approximately 8 minutes and 20 seconds to reach Earth from the Sun!

The Sun converts about 4.5 million tons of matter into energy every second!
